question: How do I join the Baltimore Dockers?
---

The easiest way is to reach out by our [contact form](http://baltimoredockers.com/contact.html).

You can also send us a message via our [instagram](https://www.instagram.com/bmoredockers/). 

Or you can come to any one of our practices, games or social events and meet the team!
