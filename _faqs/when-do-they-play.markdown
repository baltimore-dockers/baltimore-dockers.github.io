The Dockers compete from early spring through October. During the season we participate in divisional games against teams from the east coast including Washington DC, Boston, New York, North Carolina, and Philadelphia. Additionally, the team will travel to play against teams in other areas of the United States. Every season concludes with the USAFL National tournament which is hosted in a different city every year.
